✅ CANDLESTICK CHARTS
   └─ Interactive 30-day price action visualization
   └─ Green candles = Bullish days (price went up)
   └─ Red candles = Bearish days (price went down)
   └─ Hover tooltips show OHLC + Volume data
   └─ Available on Dashboard & Playground

✅ TECHNICAL ANALYSIS PANEL
   └─ Support & Resistance levels for trading zones
   └─ 50-day & 200-day Moving Averages for trends
   └─ RSI indicator for overbought/oversold conditions
   └─ MACD signal for bullish/bearish confirmation
   └─ Trend strength and volatility assessment
   └─ Trading recommendations (Buy/Hold/Accumulate)

✅ HISTORICAL EVENTS TIMELINE
   └─ Chronological list of past market events
   └─ Earnings announcements (Yellow badge)
   └─ Macro economic news (Blue badge)
   └─ Regulatory announcements (Purple badge)
   └─ Crypto-specific news (Orange badge)
   └─ Impact colors: Green (positive), Red (negative), Gray (neutral)

✅ NEWS-TO-PRICE CORRELATION
   └─ Educational explanation of how news affects prices
   └─ Shows correlation between events and chart movements
   └─ Helps understand market drivers
   └─ Learn from historical patterns

 CANDLESTICK CHART GUIDE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

What the Chart Shows:
  • 30 days of historical price data
  • Open, High, Low, Close prices (OHLC)
  • Trading volume (activity level)

Reading Candles:
  🟢 GREEN CANDLE = Bullish (price went UP that day)
     └─ Body: From open to close (higher)
     └─ Wick: From low to high

  🔴 RED CANDLE = Bearish (price went DOWN that day)
     └─ Body: From open to close (lower)
     └─ Wick: From low to high

Tooltip (Hover to See):
  • Date: Trading date
  • Open: Starting price
  • High: Peak price reached
  • Low: Lowest price reached
  • Close: Ending price
  • Volume: Number of shares traded (millions)

 TECHNICAL ANALYSIS METRICS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

SUPPORT & RESISTANCE:
  Support Level
    └─ Price "floor" where buyers jump in
    └─ Price bounces higher from this level
    └─ Example: Support at $182 = Don't expect below $182

  Resistance Level
    └─ Price "ceiling" where sellers step in
    └─ Price bounces lower from this level
    └─ Example: Resistance at $190.50 = Hard to go above $190.50

MOVING AVERAGES (MAs):
  50-Day MA
    └─ Average price of last 50 days
    └─ Shows SHORT-TERM trend
    └─ Above 50-Day MA = Uptrend

  200-Day MA
    └─ Average price of last 200 days
    └─ Shows LONG-TERM trend
    └─ Above 200-Day MA = Healthy long-term uptrend

RELATIVE STRENGTH INDEX (RSI):
  Scale: 0 to 100
  
  RSI > 70 (Overbought) ⚠️
    └─ Price has gone up too fast
    └─ Risk of pullback or correction
    └─ Consider taking profits

  RSI 30-70 (Neutral) ✓
    └─ Normal market conditions
    └─ No extreme readings

  RSI < 30 (Oversold) 💡
    └─ Price has gone down too far
    └─ Risk of bounce higher
    └─ Potential buying opportunity

MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence):
  Bullish ✓ (Green)
    └─ Uptrend confirmed
    └─ Positive momentum
    └─ Good signal to buy

  Bearish ✗ (Red)
    └─ Downtrend confirmed
    └─ Negative momentum
    └─ Good signal to sell or avoid

 HISTORICAL EVENTS GUIDE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

EVENT CATEGORIES:

  🟡 EARNINGS (Yellow)
    └─ Company quarterly/annual results
    └─ Revenue, profit, guidance updates
    └─ Usually big market movers

  🔵 MACRO (Blue)
    └─ Federal Reserve decisions
    └─ Inflation, unemployment, GDP data
    └─ Affects broad market sentiment

  🟣 REGULATORY (Purple)
    └─ SEC approvals/rejections
    └─ Policy changes
    └─ Legal decisions

  🟠 CRYPTO (Orange)
    └─ Bitcoin/Ethereum specific news
    └─ Network upgrades, partnerships
    └─ Regulation announcements

IMPACT COLORS:

  🟢 GREEN = POSITIVE IMPACT
    └─ Bullish catalyst
    └─ Price likely to go UP
    └─ Good news for the asset

  🔴 RED = NEGATIVE IMPACT
    └─ Bearish catalyst
    └─ Price likely to go DOWN
    └─ Bad news for the asset

  ⚪ GRAY = NEUTRAL IMPACT
    └─ Mixed signals
    └─ Could go either way
    └─ Requires further analysis

 USING ANALYSIS TO MAKE PREDICTIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

BULLISH SIGNALS (Predict PRICE UP):
  ✓ Price above both 50-day and 200-day MA
  ✓ MACD is bullish (uptrend)
  ✓ Support level holding (price bounces from support)
  ✓ RSI 30-70 (not overbought)
  ✓ Recent positive earnings or good news
  ✓ Multiple green candles in a row

BEARISH SIGNALS (Predict PRICE DOWN):
  ✗ Price below both 50-day and 200-day MA
  ✗ MACD is bearish (downtrend)
  ✗ Resistance level failing (can't break above)
  ✗ RSI > 70 (overbought, pullback likely)
  ✗ Recent negative earnings or bad news
  ✗ Multiple red candles in a row

NEUTRAL SIGNALS (Predict NO CHANGE):
  = Price between support and resistance
  = MACD is neutral (no clear trend)
  = No recent major events
  = RSI in middle range (30-70)
  = Mixed candle colors
